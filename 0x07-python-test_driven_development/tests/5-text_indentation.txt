==================================
This module replaces all ".", "?" and ":" with a newline in a given text

VALID INPUTS
=============
..
..
        >>> text_indentation = __import__('5-text_indentation').text_indentation
        >>> text_indentation("I am a boy. Do you love women? Ofcourse I do: cool!")
        I am a boy.
        <BLANKLINE>
        Do you love women?
        <BLANKLINE>
        Ofcourse I do:
        <BLANKLINE>
	cool!
	
	>>> text_indentation(
	...		"I am a boy. Do you love women?  Ofcourse I do: cool!."
	... )
	I am a boy.
        <BLANKLINE>
        Do you love women?
        <BLANKLINE>
        Ofcourse I do:
        <BLANKLINE>
        cool!.

	>>> text_indentation(
        ...             "I am a boy.. Do you love women?.  Ofcourse I do:?."
	...		" cool!."
        ... )
        I am a boy.
        <BLANKLINE>
        Do you love women?
        <BLANKLINE>
        Ofcourse I do:
        <BLANKLINE>
        cool!.

	>>> text_indentation("   ") 	

	>>> text_indentation(
        ...             "I am a boy..Do you love women?.  Ofcourse I do:?."
        ...             " cool!."
        ... )
        I am a boy.
        <BLANKLINE>
        Do you love women?
        <BLANKLINE>
        Ofcourse I do:
        <BLANKLINE>
        cool!.
